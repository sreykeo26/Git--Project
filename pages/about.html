Our Story

BeanBlossom Coffee has been around since 2023 in the heart of Phnom Penh.

Quality First Direct Trade Sustainable

Mission & Values

We exist to serve exceptional coffee, treat people like family, and uplift local farmers through fair, direct relationships.

  • Hospitality with heart
  • Transparency from farm to cup
  • Eco-friendly operations

Our Coffee Sourcing

We partner with smallholder farms in Mondulkiri, Vietnam’s Central Highlands, and Ethiopia’s Guji. Roasted weekly, always fresh.

Details Origins & roast profiles
  • Mondulkiri — honey process, medium roast
  • Central Highlands — washed, light roast
  • Guji — natural, light-medium roast

  • 2021 — Flagship café
  • 2023 — Roastery expansion
